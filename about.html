About

A beautiful location...

Situated in the highly sought after area of SW1, Fizzo Bar and Lounge embraces its past by combining an edgy decor with an expensive touch. The bar serves as an impressive centerpiece which lights up the room, while the more intimate lounge area provides the perfect atmosphere for those looking for a more exclusive evening. Our doors are open every day from 12:00 until 15:00 for lunch and from 17:00 until 00:00 for dinner.

and exquisite food and drink...

If you come to Fizzo clued up, you come for our cocktails. We offer a range of recipes unique to us which have been carefully curated with both taste and aesthetic in mind. Our Featured board changes each week with a new tantalising cocktail variation added to our repetoire - just ask at the bar. Meanwhile, there is no better way to compliment your evening than our carefully crafted tapas dishes. Our selection caters for everyone, with meat, fish, vegetarian and vegan choices.

come together to create the perfect experience.

Fizzo's chic and stylish ambience makes it the perfect place whatever the occasion. A spontaneous drink after work with colleagues is a suggestion that will never be turned down or you can book a table in the lounge for a relaxed bite to eat with friends. Contact us directly to arrange hosting larger event with us and we will be happy to accommodate you. We always recommend booking in advance to avoid disappointment.
